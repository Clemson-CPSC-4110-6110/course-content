Free VR Asset Resources

CPSC 4110/6110: Virtual Reality Systems

Getting Started: This guide organizes free asset resources by category. Start with Unity Asset Store and one or two resources from each category based on your project needs. Always check license terms before using assets in your projects.

üéÆUnity-Specific Resources

Unity Asset Store

Your first stop. Filter by "Free Assets" to find 3D models, textures, audio, scripts, and VR-specific tools. Look for "Starter Assets" packages.

Recommended Free Packages

AllSky Free

Collection of 10 high-quality skyboxes (200+ in paid version). Essential for establishing atmosphere in VR environments.

Kevin Iglesias Basic Motions FREE

Professional animation pack with common character movements. Works great with Mixamo characters for adding life to VR scenes.

Nature Starter Kit 1 & 2

Trees, rocks, grass, and natural environment assets optimized for performance. Perfect for outdoor VR experiences.

DOTween (HOTween v2)

Powerful tweening library for smooth animations and transitions. Essential for animating UI, objects, and camera movements in VR with minimal code.

Graphy - Ultimate Stats Monitor

Real-time FPS counter, memory usage, and performance metrics overlay. Critical for VR optimization‚Äîmaintaining frame rate is essential for comfort.

Lean Pool

Object pooling system for better VR performance. Reuses objects instead of constantly creating/destroying them, reducing frame stutters.

Quick Outline

Simple object outlining shader for highlighting interactive objects. Great for visual feedback when players look at or interact with VR objects.

Unity Particle Pack

Free particle effects system for VR environments.

Low-poly assets perform better in VR

Kenney.nl

Extensive poly-art kits and modular pieces. Public domain license. Excellent starting point for stylized VR projects.

Quaternius

Curated low-poly asset packs with characters, environments, and props. Optimized for VR performance.

Poly Pizza

Searchable collection of individual low-poly models. Great for prototyping VR scenes quickly.

Mixamo

Rigged characters with animations. Requires free Adobe account. Essential for adding animated characters to VR.

OpenGameArt.org

Community-driven eclectic mix of game assets. Varied licenses and styles.

üí° Pro Tips

  • Performance First: VR requires higher framerates. Use low-poly models when possible and test on your target hardware.
  • License Check: Always verify license terms (CC0, CC-BY, etc.) before using assets in your project.
  • Audio Matters: Don't neglect spatial audio‚Äîit's essential for presence and immersion in VR.
  • Start Small: Don't download everything at once. Pick 2-3 resources per category based on your project style.
